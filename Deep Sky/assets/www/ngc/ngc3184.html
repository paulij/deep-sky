NGC3184: Spiral Galaxy in Ursa Major

Face on spiral galaxy of type Sbc. Magnitude 10.4. About 40 million ly away.

Lies about 1° west of Tania Australis (μ UMa). As it is face on, surface brightness is low and it is hard to see.
