NGC2841: Spiral Galaxy in Ursa Major

Unbarred spiral. Magnitude 10.1. Distance 45 million ly. Large population of young blue stars and few HII regions. Large galaxy, 150,000 ly across.

Lies about 2° just south of west of θ UMa and makes a small triangle with two other 6th magnitude stars, HIP 45836 and HIP 46168. Visible in a small telescope.
