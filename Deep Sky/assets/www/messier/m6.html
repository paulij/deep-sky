M6: Butterfly Cluster in Scorpius

Open Cluster. Magniture: 4.2, About 1,600 ly away. Diameter 12 ly.

About 100 million years old. So quite young. Most of the bright stars are hot, blue B type stars. The K type orange giant star BM Scorpii is variable and at maximum magnitude of 5.8 becomes the brightest star in the cluster.

A small cluster, contains about 80 stars.
