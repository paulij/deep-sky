M59: Elliptical Galaxy in Virgo

Elliptical galaxy at 60 million ly. . Magnitude 9.6. 90,000 ly across. One of three galaxies in a row, M58, M59 and M60, about 4 ° west of Vindemiatrix.

Class E5. E means elliptical. 5 denotes the degree of ellipticity on the sky. 0 for spherical. 7 for highly elliptical.

M59 is thought to possess over 2000 globular clusters.
