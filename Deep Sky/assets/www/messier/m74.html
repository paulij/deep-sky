M74: Spiral Galaxy in Pisces

Magnitude 9.4. Type Sc. Face on spiral so has low surface brightness and is quite difficult to see.

Contains lots of clusters of young blue stars and (pink coloured) star forming regions.

Brightest member of the M74 group which also contains a number of small irregular galaxies.
