M66: Barred Spiral Galaxy in Leo

Magnitude 8.9. Class SBb. 32 million ly away.

M66 is much more active than M65 and contains lots of blue star clusters and star formation regions. Also the arms are deformed. Both are most likely caused by interactions with another galaxy, probably NGC3628, the third galaxy in the Leo Triplet.
