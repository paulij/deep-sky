M110: Dwarf Spheroidal Galaxy in Andromeda

Magnitude 8.9. A satellite of the Andromeda Galaxy it is on the opposite side of Andromeda to M32 and further away.

M110 contains some dust and possibly recent star formation. This is unusual for dwarf elliptical galaxies.
