M79: Globular Cluster in Lepus

Magnitude 7.7. 40,000 ly away. 60,000 ly from the galactic center.

This globular cluster might originally have belonged to the Canis Major Dwarf galaxy which is currently undergoing an encounter with the Milky Way. (This is like M54 which is believed to originally belong to the dwarf Sagittarius galaxy.)

Found about 4 ° south of beta Leporus (Nihal).
