M36: Open Cluster in Auriga

First of three open clusters in Auriga, the others being M37 and M38. In fact M36 is in the middle.

Magnitude 6.3. 4,100 ly away. About 14 ly across. Around 25 million years old, very much the youngest of the three. Hence it has no red giants.

Has about 60 members. The brightest are magnitude 9 B2 stars.
