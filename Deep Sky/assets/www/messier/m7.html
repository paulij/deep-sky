M7: Ptomely Cluster in Scorpius

Open cluster. Magniturd 3.3. One of the brightest Messier objects. Contains about 750 stars. The brightest star is of magnitude 5.6. Lies about 980 ly away. Has a diameter of 25 ly. Total mass about 735 times the mass of the sun.

About 200 million years old. So older than M6.

Declination of -35°, this is the southern most Messier object. Effectively cannot be seen at latitudes much north of 50°
