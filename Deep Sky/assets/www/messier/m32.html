M32: Dwarf Galaxy in Andromeda

A satellite galaxy of the Andromeda galaxy. About 6,500 ly in diameter (compared to 100,000 ly for the Milky Way).

An elliptical galaxy, it contains mostly older stars with little gas and dust and no star formation.

It contains a super-massive black hole of between 1.5 and 5 million solar masses.
