Globular Cluster in Aquarius

Magnitude 9.3. Class IX. 54,000 ly away, well beyond the galactic center. One of the most distant globular clusters. Has a retrograde orbit so perhaps was captured from a companion galaxy.

Because it is so far away, the brightest star in it is only magnitude 14.2

Found 3 ° south and just to the east of the star epsilon Aquarii (Albali).
