M69: Globular Cluster in Sagittarius

Magnitude 7.6. 30,000 ly away with a diameter of 80 ly.

Close (in the sky) to M70 which is at a similar distance and physically quite close. Also it is only a few degrees from M54, but M54 is about three times as far away and beyond the galactic center.
