M17: Omega Nebula in Sagittarius

Also known as the "Swan Nebula", "Checkmark Nebula", "Lobster Nebula",

Magnitude 6.0. An Active star forming region in Sagittarius. A distance of between 5,000 and 6,000ly places M17 beside M16 in the Sagittarius Arm of the Milky Way.

The size of the bright part of the nebula is about 15ly. The total size of the gas cloud is about 40 ly with a total mass of about 800 solar masses. This makes it larger than the Orion nebula.

M17 contains a star cluster of about 35 stars which are not obvious in optical images.
