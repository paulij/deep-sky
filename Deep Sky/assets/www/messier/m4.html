M4: Globular Cluster in Scorpius

Magnitude 7. About 7000 ly away (one of the closest). Also quite close to the galactic plane. Diameter 70 ly. Class IX globular cluster so low concentration of stars.

Has over 100,000 stars and a mass of about 67,000 times the mass of the sun.

Passes close to the galactic center on its orbit (5kpc) so has probably been stripped of a lot of its stars

1.5 degrees west of Antares so easy to find.
