M44: Beehive Cluster in Cancer

Also known as Praesepe. Magnitude 3.7. Visible to the naked eye. 200 stars have been confirmed as members of the cluster.

577 ly away. Age about 730 million years. Similar in age to the Hyades. Both might have originated in a huge nebule 700-800 million years ago.
