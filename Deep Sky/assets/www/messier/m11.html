M11: Wild Duck Cluster in Scutum

Open Cluster. Magnitude 6.3. One of the richest and most compact open clusters. Similar density of stars to a globular cluster of class X (2.4 stars per cubic ly.)

A distance of 6120 ly places it in the Sagittarius arm of the Milky Way.

2,900 stars attributed to the cluster. The brightest stars are of magnitude 10 apart from one which is of magnitude 8.

About 250 million years old.
