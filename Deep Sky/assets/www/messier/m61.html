M61: Spiral Galaxy in Virgo

About 100,000 ly across so similar in size to the Milky Way. Magntude 9.6. About 8° south of the main cluster of galaxies.

The galaxy contains lots of star forming regions. Also its spiral structure is not very regular. Both of these could be caused by interaction with some nearby smaller galaxies.
