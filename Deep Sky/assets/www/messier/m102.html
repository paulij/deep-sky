M102: Spiral Galaxy in Draco

There is some debate about this entry in the Messier Catalog. Some believe it is a duplicate of M101 whicle some suggest it is NGC5866 in Draco.

NGC5866

Magnitude 10.7. About 50m ly away. Also know as the Spindle galaxy.

Has been classified as either a lenticular or spiral galaxy. The prominent dust lanes are more consistent with it being an edge on spiral galaxy.

The main galaxy in the small NGC5866 group which also contains the spirals NGC5879 and NGC5907.
