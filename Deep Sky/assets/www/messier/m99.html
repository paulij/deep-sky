M99: Spiral Galaxy in Coma Berenices

About 50 million ly away. Magnitude 10.4.

In 2000 astronomers discovered a gas cloud close to M99 called VIRGOHI21. It seems to exerting a large pull on M99 causing one of its spiral arms to be more less tightly wound that the other. Some astronomers have concluded that VIRGOHI21 is a dark galaxy. A dark galaxy is a (hypothetical) galaxy consisting of dark matter.

M99 was one of the first galaxies in which a spiral was detected (by Lord Rosse in 1864).
