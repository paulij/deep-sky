M13: Great Globular Cluster in Hercules

Magnitude 5.8. Probably the best globular cluster in the northern hemisphere. One third of the way down the right hand side of the keystone asterism in Hercules. Great object for a small telescope.

About 145 ly years in diameter. 600,000 solar masses.

The Arecibos message was broadcast towards this globular cluster in 1974. Designed to communicate the existence of human life to extraterrestrials.

Frank Drake (of the famous Drake equation), Carl Sagan and a number of others composed the message.
