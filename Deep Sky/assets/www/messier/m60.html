M60: Elliptical Galaxy in Virgo

One of the giant elliptical galaxies in the Virgo cluster. Again at 60 million ly. Class E2. Magnitude 8.8. 120,000 ly across.

The east most of three galaxies in a row, M58, M59 and M60, about 4 ° west of Vindemiatrix.

There is a nearby galaxy NGC 4647 (just visible in the image) which does not seem to be interacting with M60 so probably lies at a different distance.

M60 is known to contain a super massive black hole of maybe 4 billion solar masses. The accretion disk around the black hole is relatively quiet.
