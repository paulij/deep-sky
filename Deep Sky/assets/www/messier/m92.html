M92: Globular Cluster in Hercules

Magnitude 6.3. About 27,000 ly away. Another fine globular cluster in Hercules (as well as M13).

The celestial north pole moves with a period of about 26,000 years. At one stage in the cycle, the north pole is within one degree of M92, making it effectively the marker for due north. This wont happen for another 14,000 years though so don't wait up.
