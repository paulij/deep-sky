M109: Spiral Galaxy in Ursa Major

Barred spiral. Magnitude 10.6. Distance 67 million ly, the furthest galaxy in the Messier catalog. It is large at about 137,000 ly in diameter.

M109 is the brightest galaxy in the M109 group, a large galaxy group containing maybe 50 galaxies.

Less than one degree south east of Phecda, the bottom left star in the plough asterism. There is another magnitude 10 galaxy, NGC 3953, about 1 ° south west of M109 and part of the M109 galaxy group.
