M101: Pinwheel Galaxy in Ursa Major

About 21 million ly away. Magnitude 7.9. Another large galaxy, with diameter of 170 million km.

Small bulge containing only about 3% of the total mass of the galaxy. Contains a large number of large and bright HII star forming regions.

M101, together with a number of companion galaxies, form the M101 galaxy group.
