M24: Sagittarius Star Cloud in Sagittarius

Also know as "Delle Caustiche".

A large area of the sky 1.5 ° across and south of M17 and M18. Not a star cluster as such, more a particularly bright area of the sky due to a chance gap/tunnel in the interstellar dust in the Milky Way.
