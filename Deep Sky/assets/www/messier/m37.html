M37: Open Cluster in Auriga

The second of the three clusters in Auriga, about 3 ° east south east of M36.

Magnitude 6.2. 4,200 ly away. Contains up to 500 stars so by far the largest of the thre clusters.

About 300 million years old so far older than M36. It contains a least a dozen red giants.
