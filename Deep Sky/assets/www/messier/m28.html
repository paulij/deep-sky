M28: Globular Cluster in Sagittarius

Magnitude 6.8. About 18,000 ly away with a diameter of about 60 ly. Mass 500,000 solar masses.

Not quite as impressive as its neighbour M22.

Found less than a degree north west of Kaus Borealis (3rd mag.).
