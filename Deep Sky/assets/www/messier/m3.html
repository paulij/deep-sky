M3: Globular Cluster in Canes Venatici

Magniture 5.9. About 34,000 ly away. and 190 ly in diameter. Contains over 500,000 stars and has a mass of about 800,000 suns.

Contains 274 variable stars, the highest number by far in a Globular Cluster

Lies between Arcturus and Cor Caroli which are some distance apart in a quite 'dark' part of the sky.
