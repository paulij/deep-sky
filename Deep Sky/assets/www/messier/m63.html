M63: The Sunflower Galaxy in Canes Venatici

Magnitude 8.6. About 27 million ly away. Class Sb. It is slightly smaller than the Milky Way galaxy.

Although M63 is 6° south of M51 it has been classified as part of the M51 group.

There is a dark band of dust to the south of the galaxy (up in the image).
