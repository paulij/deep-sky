M105: Elliptical Galaxy in Leo

Magnitude 10.2. About 38m ly away.

Contains a supermassive black hole of between 140 and 200 million solar masses. Also contains a few young stars and star clusters, showing that elliptical galaxies can create new stars, but very slowly.

Part of the M96 group of galaxies which also contains M95 and NGC 3384. A relatively close up view of M105 shows NGC 3384 and NGC 3389. NGC 3384 is physically close by while NGC 3389 is a background galaxy.
