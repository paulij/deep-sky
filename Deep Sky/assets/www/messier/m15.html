M15: Globular Cluster in Pegasus

Magnitude 7.6. 33,500 ly away and 175 ly in diameter.

The cluster may have suffered a "core collapse". At the center of the cluster, inter stellar distances can be as small as the size of the solar system, i.e. the distance from the sun to Pluto. There is also the possibility of a intermediate mass black hole at the center of the cluster.

Follow from Biham to Enif and approximately half the distance again to come to M15.
