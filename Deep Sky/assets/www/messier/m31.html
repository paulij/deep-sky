M31: Andromeda Galaxy in Andromeda

Magnitude 3.4. About 2.6 million ly away. The largest of three large spiral galaxies in our local group, the others being the Milky Way and the Triangulum Galaxy.

Contains about 1 trillion stars, about twice as mnay as out Milky Way Galaxy. However recent studies suggest that it might not be more massive than the Milky Way due to it containing less dark matter.

In the image the dwark galaxy M32 is seen just above M31 and the dwarf galaxy M110 is seen below and further away.
