M29: Open Cluster in Cygnus

Magnitude 7.1. Found in a fairly crowded part of the Milky Way. A couple of degrees south and just to the east of Sadr.

Distance is hard to estimate due to unknown light absorption due to dust. Estimates range from 4,000 to 7,000 ly. Age 10 million years so quite young. Its hottest stars are B0 giants.
