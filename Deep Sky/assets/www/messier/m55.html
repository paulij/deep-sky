M55:Globular Cluster in Sagittarius

Magnitude 6.3. About 17,000 ly away. Mass about 300,000 solar masses so quite small.

Was discovered by Nicolas Louis de Lacaille from Sout Africa. It took some time before Messier was able to see it due to its low declination.
