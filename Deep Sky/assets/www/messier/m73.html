M73: Four Stars in Aquarius

Another oddity in the Messier catalogue. Messier saw a cluster of star with some nebulosity.

There is some disagreement about whether this is a open cluster or not. All four stars are evolved giants and are quite close.

Found just to the east of M72, about 3° south and 3° east of the star epsilon Aquarii (Albali).
