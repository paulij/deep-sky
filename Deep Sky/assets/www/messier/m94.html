M94: Spiral Galaxy in Canes Venatici

Magnitude 9.0. About 16 million ly. The brightest galaxy in the M94 group which contains about 20 galaxies.

The galaxy is seen to contain two rings. Both are locations of active star formation.

A recent study has shown the galaxy to contain practically no dark matter. This is a controversial result and has not yet been widely accepted.

The image is an infrared and ultraviolet composite image.

Image Credit: R. Jay GaBany, Spitzer Legacy Program, GALEX Nearby Galaxy Survey
