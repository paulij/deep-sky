M70: Globular Cluster in Sagittarius

Magnitude 7.9. 30,000 ly away and close to M69. M70 has a dense core and has suffered a core collapse.

In 1995 the comet Hale-Bopp was discoved by Alan Hale and Thomas Bopp as they were observing this globular.
