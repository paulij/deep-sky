M20: Triffid Nebula in Sagittarius

Magnitude 8.5. A round H II region with a (blue) reflection nebula to the north. A number of very dark dust lanes run through the nebula.

An O7 star lies near the center of the nebula. It is the the brightest of a number of very bright stars similar to the Trapezium in M42.

The Spitzer Space Telescope has discovered 30 embryonic stars and 120 newborn stars in the nebula. These are not seen in visible light images.

The region is younger than M42, less that half a million years old.
