M26: Open Cluster in Scutum

Magnitude 8.0. A small neighbour of M11 (Wild Duck Cluster). About 5,000ly away. Diameter 22 ly.

Brightest star is a magnitude 11.9 B8 star. There is a faint ring around the center of the cluster. This is probably due to dust than an actual lack of stars.
