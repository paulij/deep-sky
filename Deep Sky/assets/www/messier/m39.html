M39: Open Cluster in Cygnus

Magnitude 4.6. A large and loose open cluster consisting of about 30 stars. About 800 ly away. 7 ly in diameter.

Age is between 230 and 300 million years old.

About 9 ° east and 3 ° north of Deneb. Stands out even in a rich part of the Milky Way.
