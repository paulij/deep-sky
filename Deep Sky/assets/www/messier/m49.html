M49: Elliptical Galaxy in Virgo

The first member of the Virgo group of galaxies catalogued by Messier in 1771.

Magnitude 8.4. About 60 million ly away. Type E4. One of the giant elliptical galaxies in the Virgo Group, the others being M87 and M60.

Forms part of the smaller Virgo B subcluster located 4.5 ° away from the main cluster.

Has an estimated 5,900 globular clusters.
